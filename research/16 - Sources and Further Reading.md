# Sources and Further Reading

## Annotated Bibliography of Authoritative Sources

This document provides references to the key sources used in this knowledge base, with annotations on their value and relevance.

## Consulting and Industry Research

### KPMG
**"2025 Global CEO Outlook" (October 2025)**
- Survey of 1,350 CEOs across 11 markets
- Key findings on AI investment, leadership capabilities, workforce transformation
- Valuable for current CEO sentiment and priorities
- URL: https://kpmg.com/xx/en/our-insights/value-creation/global-ceo-outlook-survey.html

### Russell Reynolds Associates
**"The AI Leadership Opportunity—and How to Unlock It" (November 2025)**
- Focused on infrastructure companies and AI-ready leadership
- Articulates four capabilities: learning agility, systems thinking, potential over past, AI learner
- Strong on what distinguishes future-ready leaders
- URL: https://www.russellreynolds.com/en/insights/articles/the-ai-leadership-opportunity-and-how-to-unlock-it

### Egon Zehnder
**"Assessing AI Skills in Leadership: Why It Has Become Critical" (September 2025)**
- Universal AI leadership framework
- Distinguishes AI Transformers (business) from AI Builders (technical)
- Seven-point assessment scale
- Practical for assessment design
- URL: https://www.egonzehnder.com/industries/technology-communications/artificial-intelligence/insights/assessing-ai-skills-in-leadership-why-it-has-become-critical-for-business-leaders

### Bain & Company
**"88% of Business Transformations Fail" (April 2024)**
- Transformation failure research
- Finding that 90% of value from 5% of roles
- Emphasis on critical role identification
- URL: https://www.bain.com/about/media-center/press-releases/2024/88-of-business-transformations-fail-to-achieve-their-original-ambitions-those-that-succeed-avoid-overloading-top-talent/

### McKinsey & Company
**"Common Pitfalls in Transformations" (March 2022)**
- Interview format explaining why transformations fail
- 70% failure rate citation
- Practical transformation guidance
- URL: https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/transformation/our-insights/common-pitfalls-in-transformations-a-conversation-with-jon-garcia

## Leadership and Competency Research

### AACSB/CEMS
**"What Next-Level Skills Will Leaders Need by 2030?" (December 2025)**
- Qualitative research with 20 global companies
- Five competencies: Analytical+Creative, Entrepreneurial, Cultural Intelligence, Depth+Breadth, Digital Fluency
- Good for future capability requirements
- URL: https://www.aacsb.edu/insights/articles/2025/12/what-next-level-skills-will-leaders-need-by-2030

### Jacob Morgan
**"The Future Leader" (2020)**
- Based on 140 CEO interviews
- "Notable Nine" framework (4 mindsets + 5 skills)
- Survey of 14,000 employees with LinkedIn
- URL: https://thefutureorganization.com/140-ceos-on-what-it-will-take-to-be-a-leader-in-2030/

### SI Labs
**"Why Business Transformations Fail: 6 Patterns" (March 2026)**
- Six failure patterns from research and practice
- Heifetz adaptive vs. technical framework
- Practical diagnostic approach
- URL: https://www.si-labs.com/en/articles/why-transformations-fail/

## Assessment and Learning Agility

### Frontiers in Psychology
**"Development and Validation of the Leadership Learning Agility Scale" (December 2022)**
- Academic validation research
- Construct development and measurement
- Limitations discussion
- URL: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2022.991299/full

### Cogn-IQ.org
**"How to Assess Learning Agility in Hiring (Evidence-Based)" (October 2025)**
- Critical analysis of learning agility construct
- Finding that GMA + Openness explain much of learning agility
- Practical assessment recommendations
- URL: https://www.cogn-iq.org/blog/how-to-assess-learning-agility/

### Korn Ferry
**"Leadership Architect™ Global Competency Framework"**
- 38 competencies across 12 clusters
- Four dimensions: Competencies, Experiences, Traits, Drivers
- Industry standard reference
- URL: https://www.kornferry.com/capabilities/talent-suite-hcm-software/korn-ferry-assess/leadership-architect

### DDI (Development Dimensions International)
**"Leadership Competencies: The Foundation of Talent Management"**
- 114 competencies across 10 job families
- Success Profiles methodology
- Behavioural focus
- URL: https://www.ddi.com/solutions/leadership-competencies

## Cultural Leadership Research

### PMC/NCBI
**"Western and Non-Western Leadership Styles and Employee Wellbeing" (2024)**
- Study in high-power distance context (Russia)
- Comparison of transformational, authoritarian, paternalistic
- Important for cultural critique
- URL: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10834627/

### ResearchGate
**"Cultural Context of Leadership" (March 2022)**
- Six premises of Western theory not applicable to non-Western contexts
- Critical for Western model critique
- URL: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/358961092_Cultural_Context_of_Leadership

### ResearchGate
**"Leadership Styles Across Cultures: Western and Asian" (July 2024)**
- Hofstede and Deleuzian analysis
- Comparative study of leadership approaches
- URL: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/382206570_Leadership_Styles_Across_Cultures_A_Comparative_Study_of_Western_and_Asian_Contexts_Through_Hofested_and_Deleuzian_Lenses
